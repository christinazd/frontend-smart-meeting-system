Smart Meeting Room & Minutes Management System

Our project is designed to streamline meetings for organizations, ensuring efficiency and clarity. It helps users schedule rooms, manage participants, and track discussions effortlessly.

The system provides a centralized platform where all meeting information, documents, and minutes are stored digitally, making it easy to access and review anytime.

With features like automated reminders, collaborative notes, and real-time updates, our system reduces manual work and ensures every meeting is productive and well-documented.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify and optimize the way organizations manage meetings. We aim to provide a user-friendly platform where users can easily book meeting rooms, invite attendees, track minutes, and generate actionable summaries. The system ensures efficiency, transparency, and productivity for all stakeholders.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading solution for meeting management by integrating modern technology with organizational workflows. We aspire to create a seamless experience where meetings are well-organized, decisions are clearly documented, and collaboration is enhanced across teams.
